Projects About Me

Alex Shank

B.S. Electrical and Computer Engineering

Alex was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio and attended Saint Ignatius High School before starting at The Ohio State University in the fall of 2016. Majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering, he has taken elective courses focusing on General Business, Computer Architecture, and Digital Signal Processing. Throughout his undergraduate studies, Alex has completed internships with Lincoln Electric (a global welding company), RoviSys (an automation and information solutions company), and CapTech (a technology consulting firm). In these positions, Alex has been exposed to manufacturing, electrical hardware R&D, robotics, software development, component sourcing, and iterative design.
